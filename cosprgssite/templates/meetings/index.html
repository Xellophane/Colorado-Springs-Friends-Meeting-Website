    Meeting for Worship for Business

    The term monthly meeting has two meanings. Primary, it refers to the fundamental unit of the Society of Friends; it also reflects the practice of coming together for meeting for worship with a concern for business, usually occurs once a month.

    IMYM Faith and Practice page 56

    The Colorado Springs Friends Meeting currently has business meeting on the third First Day (or Sunday) of the month.
